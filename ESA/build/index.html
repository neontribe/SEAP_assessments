SEAP Assessments : ESA

Question: Can you move around and use steps without difficulty?

Question: What is the furthest you can move safely on level ground without needing to stop?

Question: After moving once, how far can you move again within a short time without it causing you significant discomfort or exhaustion?

Question: Can you go up or down two steps without being helped by another person even with the support of a handrail?

Question: Can you stand and sit without difficulty?

Question: If two seats are placed next to each other could you move from one to the other without help from someone else?

Question: How long could you either sit or stand or both at a workstation for the majority of time without help from someone else before it caused you significant discomfort or exhaustion and you had to move away?

Question: Can you reach up with both your arms without difficulty?

Question: Can you pick up and move things without any difficulty?

Question: Can you pick up and move a half litre (pint) carton of liquid?

Question: Can you pick up and move a litre (2 pint) carton of liquid?

Question: Can you pick up and move a light but bulky object like an empty cardboard box?

Question: Can you use one hand to press a button for example on a telephone key pad?

Question: Can you turn the pages of a book with one or both hands?

Question: Can you use one hand to pick up a pound coin or something similar with one or both hands?

Question: Can you use one hand to operate a computer keyboard or mouse (it does not matter whether or not it has been specially adapted for you or you know how to use a computer)?

Question: Can you communicate with other people without any difficulty?

Question: Can you make yourself understood through speaking, writing, typing, or any other means you normally use, without help from another person?

Question: Can you give a simple message to someone such as a message explaining how dangerous something is?

Question: How difficult would you find it to pass a simple message to stranger?

Question: Can you understand other people without any difficulty?

Question: Which of these best describes how well you can understand a simple message such as the location of a fire escape?
